Maya Man (b. 1996) is a digital and generative artist whose practice spans browser interventions, algorithmic text-image systems, web projects, and hybrid installations. She interrogates notions of identity, performativity, and mediation in digital culture, especially focusing on how femininity, self-care, authenticity, and persona are constructed online. She frames the screen not merely as a passive surface but as an active site of performance and surveillance.
